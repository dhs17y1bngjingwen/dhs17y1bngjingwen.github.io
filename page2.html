<h1> My trip to the Lee Kong Chian Natural History Museum</h1>

What we did:

  • We took a tour around the museum
  • We learned about the different phylas and the class chordata
  • We did were given specimens which belonged to different phylas and we had to figure out which phyla they belong to

My feelings:

Out of all the other learning journeys, I enjoyed this one the most as our co-form teacher was with us and we also took a class photo. I love science and to be able to learn about different phylas and to learn about the class chordata is vry interesting to me and I enjoyed that a lot. And in this learning journey, we were able to stay indoors and enjoy the air-condition while on other journeys, we had to head out. I hope to have more of such learning journies in the future.
